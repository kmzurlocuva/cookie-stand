Pat has collected the following data, currently:

  • Maximum # of customers per hour
  • Minimum # of customers per hour
  • Average cookies per customer

Using these data, we can determine the following:

  • Random number (randomNum) of customers per hour using the min and max number of customers
  • Multiply the number just generated (randomNum) of customers per hour by the average cookies per customer to create the # of cookies per hour needed (cookiesNeededPerHour)
  • Make a list (array) of each hour with the number of cookies needed for baking within it.
  • Total all cookies needed per day (sum of all cookiesNeededPerHour) from the array
